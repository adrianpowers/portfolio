About Me:

In December of 2021, I graduated from East Tennessee State University with my Bachelors of Science in Digital Media with every intention to break into the gaming industry and become a 3D Character Animator. By June, I had scored my first position with an indie company as a contract animator. As I continued with this job, I realized that I had little passion for it, and it wasn't mentally engaging enough for me. I remembered the few coding classes I had taken in college before transferring to ETSU and realized that would be the better fit for me. As a future front end dev, I hope to marry the right and left brain by bringing gorgeous creative endeavors to life through logic and code, alongside my eye for design. To achieve these goals, I enrolled as a member of Thinkful's Software Engineering Immersion bootcamp as part of the October cohort.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing video games, painting and drawing, and spending time with my wife (of almost 6 years!) Abby and our two Spice Girl cats, Cinnamon and Sage.
